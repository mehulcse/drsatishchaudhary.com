Meet Dr. Satish Chaudhary

M.B.B.S, DNB (General Medicine), DrNB (Nephrology)

Dr Chaudhary is a Consultant Nephrologist and transplant physician (Kidney Specialist) Practicing in Patan Janta Hospital, Gujarat . His areas of interest are interventional Nephrology, critical care nephrology, Paediatric nephrology and kidney transplantation. He is involved in many Kidney Transplants, which include robotic transplants, paediatric transplants, ABO/HLA incompatible transplants and other high-risk transplants.

His Professional Memberships includes

  • Member of Association of Physicians of India. (API)
  • Member of Delhi Nephrology Society (DNS)
  • Member of Indian Society of Nephrology (ISN)
